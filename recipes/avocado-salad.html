Avocado Salad

Description

This avocado salad is a delicious combination of ripe avocados, sweet onions, fresh tomatoes, and cilantro. This recipe is so easy to make and very colorful â€” I think you'll like it!

Ingredients

  • Avocados
  • Onion
  • Bell pepper
  • Tomato
  • Cilantro
  • Lime
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Combine avocados, onion, bell pepper, tomato, cilantro, and lime juice in a large bowl.
  3. Gently toss until evenly coated. Season with salt and pepper.
